Building a Competition-Winning Fire Fighting Robot

In my second semester, I had the incredible opportunity to design and construct a competition-winning fire-fighting robot using Arduino. This project marked a significant milestone in my journey as an engineer and maker.

The robot was equipped with state-of-the-art sensors and a robust Arduino-based control system. It excelled in navigating challenging environments, detecting and extinguishing flames efficiently. Our team's dedication, innovative approach, and meticulous programming enabled us to clinch the top prize in the competition. This experience not only honed my technical skills but also ignited a passion for robotics that continues to drive my projects today.

Mobile Wallet App

(Award Winning)

Features:

  1. Registration and Login system: Users can register and log in securely.
  2. QR Payment and Phone Number Payment: Convenient payment methods using QR codes and phone numbers.
  3. Asymmetric Encryption: Utilizes encryption techniques similar to Bitcoin for secure communication between users and the server.
  4. My Own Virtual Bank API: Provides a virtual bank API for loading money into the wallet.
  5. KYC Verification: Implements Know Your Customer verification for user security.
  6. Pin Number: Requires a PIN number for sending money to enhance security.
  7. Custom Payment Gateway: Created a unique payment gateway integrated with a movie ticket website for seamless transactions.
